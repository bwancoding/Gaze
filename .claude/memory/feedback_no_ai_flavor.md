Website copy must not sound AI-generated. Western users will immediately bounce if they detect AI writing style.

**Why:** User's Canadian friend warned that Western audiences actively despise AI and have learned to detect AI-style writing. The current site reads too "AI-flavored."

**How to apply:**
- Never use em dashes (—) in marketing/UI copy
- Avoid flowery, philosophical, overly-inspirational language
- Don't advertise AI as a feature; hide it behind the product
- Use short, direct, human-sounding sentences
- "We Gaze Upon the Same Moon" is the user's OWN slogan, not AI - do NOT change it
- Present analysis as "multi-perspective analysis" not "AI analysis"
